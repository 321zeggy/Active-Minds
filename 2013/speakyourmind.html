Speak Your Mind, MIT!

Speak Your Mind, MIT is an ongoing project headed by Active Minds @ MIT.

We are asking for people to submit success stories, or just inspirational stories, about their own past or current struggles with mental health diseases. Upon review of the stories with our executive board, as well as MIT Mental Health professionals, we will publish these stories in a series of ad-articles in the Tech.

Please keep in mind that you can submit stories anonymously, and that the submission/publication process is rolling so you can submit a story anytime while Speak Your Mind, MIT is still running!

Please visit Speak Your Mind page to read the stories that your peers have submitted. Feel free to comment and contribute to the conversation around mental health.

Submission Instructions:

  • If you would like to submit a story non-anonymously, please send your story to speakyourmind.mit@gmail.com. Please make sure to include your name and contact information in the email!

  • If you would like to submit a story anonymously, please use the form below. This will just send the story to us without any information as to who sent it.
